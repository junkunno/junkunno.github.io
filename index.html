Why am I in data analytics?


Great Question! Truth be told I NEEDED A CHANGE! I was just tired of being in sales related roles, my degree
is Information Systems and while I never sought out a role in tech, I've always ended up self-learning
and applying that knowledge to whatever the task was at hand whether it was building websites
from scratch using Photoshop & Dreamweaver, sourcing & mastering CRM software for patient management,
or becoming a quick expert on the widely different hotel management softwares used by the different
booking platforms. --I NEEDED A CHANGE!
Cue the Google Data Analytics Certification course which I completed and thoroughly enjoyed. I know
it's just a bare-bones foundation to the universe of data analytics but it gave me the confidence
to return to the tech world in a more formal fashion.

Dear Future Employer ...

I know that my resume is 'unique' but seeing past that I hope you'd be able to see
something that I know in myself:- That I'm more than competent, sharp, and a quick
and determined learner that can take on most anything thrown at me!
Thank you,
Your future excellent hire
- Dominique
